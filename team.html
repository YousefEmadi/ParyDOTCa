We are three aviation enthusiasts from Quebec, driven by a deep passion for innovation and a commitment to making a real impact. Living in a lush, green environment, Montérégie, we recognize the urgent need to protect wildlife. With cutting-edge technology and unwavering dedication, we aim to revolutionize wildlife conservation and create lasting change.

Pary Wings Project engineering team includes experienced engineers from Canada and around the world, committed to environment protection and using their expertise to a meaningful mission for our children and next generations.

Our Mission

To protect forests, communities, and critical infrastructure from wildfires by leveraging advanced drone technologies for rapid detection, intelligent response, and effective suppression

Our Common Values: Flying with Purpose

Safety Above All

We prioritize the safety of our team, partners, and the public. Our technology and training are built to enhance safety on the front lines of firefighting.

Reliability & Precision

Our commitment to innovation and quality ensures our technology performs with precision when it's needed most.

Partnership

We believe fighting wildfires is a collective effort. We are open to collaborate with anyone in the world who "thinks green and acts green". We must work closely with firefighters and communities to develop effective solutions together.

Courage to Act

We respond to crises with courage and urgency. We are committed to providing leadership and empowering those on the front lines with the most modern tools to make swift, informed decisions.
